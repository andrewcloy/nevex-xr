# User Profiles

## tactical

- overlay_density: low to medium
- default_fusion_behavior: fusion-first with fast fallback to low-light
- target_highlighting_intensity: medium
- recording_defaults: manual record armed, snapshots easy
- warning_sensitivity: high
- clutter_tolerance: low

## hunter

- overlay_density: low
- default_fusion_behavior: low-light primary with optional thermal highlight
- target_highlighting_intensity: medium-high for animals, low for everything else
- recording_defaults: snapshot quick access, recording optional
- warning_sensitivity: medium
- clutter_tolerance: low

## police_patrol

- overlay_density: medium
- default_fusion_behavior: fusion with strong link and recording awareness
- target_highlighting_intensity: medium for person detection
- recording_defaults: recording easy to confirm, playback accessible
- warning_sensitivity: high
- clutter_tolerance: medium

## search_and_rescue

- overlay_density: medium
- default_fusion_behavior: thermal-assisted fusion
- target_highlighting_intensity: high for person and hotspot cues
- recording_defaults: recording on demand with export pathway emphasized
- warning_sensitivity: medium-high
- clutter_tolerance: medium

## recreational

- overlay_density: very low
- default_fusion_behavior: low-light primary with simple thermal toggle
- target_highlighting_intensity: low
- recording_defaults: snapshot first, recording secondary
- warning_sensitivity: medium-low
- clutter_tolerance: low
